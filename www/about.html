Mini-Cog:

A quick 3-5 minute cognitive assessment that involves the user recalling 3 words and drawing a clock using the provided time. The scores can range from 0-5. An explanation of your score will be provided on the completion of the assessment.

Alzheimers Disease:

Also known as senile demntia,it is a progressive disease that destroys memory and other mental functions. Early detection of Alzheimers can allow for treatment to be more effective. Curing this disease is not possible yet, however treatment can still help. Some symptopms of Alzheimers include confusion, disorientation, forgetfulnes, inability to concentrate and recognize common things.
